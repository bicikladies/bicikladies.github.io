We are a community of like-minded women, united by a love for bicycles and a desire to support each other ❤️

We ride mainly in Belgrade, Novi Sad, and the surrounding areas. We gather for joint rides almost every Sunday in good weather. You can find out when the next ride will be on Strava, Instagram or in our chats.

What do you need to join our rides?

Two basic things:

  • A helmet! A helmet is mandatory, and you must bring one.
  • A bicycle. Any bicycle that you are comfortable riding for 30 km or more will do. The best option is a road or a gravel bike. It will be challenging on a mountain or city rental bike.

Everyone is welcome

You can come to the ride with any level of cycling skills. We ride at a pace comfortable for everyone and don’t leave anyone behind! Each of us was a beginner once, so we know what it’s like — we’ll help and give tips.

If you need a benchmark, the average speed of our rides is 20–25 km/h, depending on wind, landscape and route. If the route has a lot of elevation gain, the average might be 17–19 km/h.

Have doubts?

If you are afraid or in doubt — write in any of our chats or on Instagram. And remember! We have several cases where girls started riding little by little, got hooked, and bought a road or a gravel bike. Consider such risks and allocate a budget 😉.
